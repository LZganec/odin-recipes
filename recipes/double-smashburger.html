Double smashburger

Back to index

Ingredients

For the patties:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Salt and pepper

For the burgers:

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup mustard
  • 1/4 cup pickles
  • 1/4 cup onions
  • 1/4 cup lettuce
  • 1/4 cup tomatoes

Instructions

1. Divide the ground beef into 8 equal portions and shape into balls.

2. Heat a cast-iron skillet over high heat until very hot.

3. Place 2 balls of ground beef in the skillet and smash with a spatula to form thin patties.

4. Season with salt and pepper and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the patties are browned and crispy.

5. Flip the patties, top each with a slice of cheese, and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.

6. Remove the patties from the skillet and repeat with the remaining ground beef.

7. Toast the hamburger buns in the skillet.

8. Assemble the burgers by spreading mayonnaise on the bottom bun, adding a patty with cheese, and topping with ketchup, mustard, pickles, onions, lettuce, and tomatoes.

9. Place the top bun on the burger and serve immediately.
