Ground beef rolls

Back to index

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp basil
  • 1/4 tsp thyme
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp mustard

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, green pepper, celery, parsley, milk, egg, salt, pepper, garlic powder, oregano, basil, thyme, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Mix well.

3. Shape the mixture into 1-inch balls and place in a baking dish.

4. In a separate bowl, combine ketchup, water, Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, and mustard. Pour over the meatballs.

5. Bake for 45 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through.

6. Serve the meatballs with rice or pasta and enjoy!
