Big Mac tacos

Back to index

Description

Big Mac tacos are a delicious and easy-to-make meal that combines the best of both worlds: the classic flavors of a Big Mac and the convenience of a taco. These tacos are perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a fun twist on taco night. With seasoned ground beef, shredded lettuce, diced pickles, shredded cheese, and a special sauce, these tacos are sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced pickles
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup Big Mac sauce (or Thousand Island dressing)

Instructions

1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned and cooked through.

2. Add the taco seasoning and water according to the package instructions. Simmer for 5 minutes.

3. Warm the tortillas in the microwave or on the stovetop.

4. Assemble the tacos by filling each tortilla with a scoop of the seasoned ground beef, shredded lettuce, diced pickles, shredded cheese, and a drizzle of Big Mac sauce.

5. Serve the tacos immediately and enjoy!
